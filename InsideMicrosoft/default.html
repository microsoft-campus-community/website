Join our digital-only student event with Microsoft!

The Microsoft Campus Community invites you to join an online conference of a different kind. Our community aims at connecting tech-enthusiastic students with experts and faciliate learning and networking. We will invite experts to give you insights into their daily business, their roles and the workings of Microsoft. In addition to that you have the opportunity to learn from the experts themselves on technical topics ranging from AI and Azure to the Modern Workplace. You are invited to connect with fellow students as well as Microsofties and ask all the questions you always wanted to ask.
