Join our online hack day

The Microsoft Campus Community invites you to join us for MLH's Local Hack Day: Share Day. Share Day is a global demo day focused on highlighting the achievements of your community. Over the course of 12 hours, attendees will put the finishing touches on their current projects and participate in a global project showcase.

This is an event for all skill levels, from beginners to the most veteran developers. Workshop attendees will learn skills that they can apply in their projects, schoolwork, job, and beyond.


11.04.2020 | 08:30 am - 09:00 pm CEST

Become part of our independent German-wide community of techies and tech-enthusiasts and engage in this interactive online event.
We are looking forward to (virtually) meeting you!
