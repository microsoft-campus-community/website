Digital Learning.
A special look at the needs of schools.

Join our interactive digital-only event series!

Discover the possibilities of digital learning in this online only event by the Microsoft Campus Community. Discover how teachers like Sabine Thierfelder and Matthias Thiede integrate tools into their teaching and expand the possibilities of learning for their students.
Dive in with us into the world of teaching and learning solutions and test out different methods and approaches to create your virtual classroom.
This event is for everyone who's interested, from teachers and professors to students or to none of the above. No prior knowledge is required.
Most of the sessions will be held in German. We will, however, provide english subtitles.
