Plan, Do, Check, Act: Retrospectives

Florentina Fuchs

Microsoft Campus Community

at 6:00 - 8:00 pm on November 12, 2020

Language: English

When we walk through our lives, we always think that our eyes are free to look ahead and we can see what is coming and act accordingly. In truth, we are more like rowers in a boat, sitting with our backs to the front. We have an excellent view of the things we just have left behind. If we look to the side, we can see where we are at the moment, which places we are passing at the moment.

We become clear about what has worked well and what we therefore want to preserve and strengthen in the future by reflecting on what we can improve and how we can avoid making mistakes in the future. And by thinking about what we want to try out, hoping that this will strengthen our future course.

By now we are already in the middle of a RETROSPECTIVE, in a continuous improvement process ... or in Kaizen ... or at Inspect and Adapt.

I invite you to deep dive into the process of a retrospective. Together and during an one hour workshop we'll explore the individual as well as organisational impact of a retrospective.
