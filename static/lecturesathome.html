Introduction to Kubernetes

Lucas Possani

DevOps Engineer at Motius

at 6:00 - 8:00 pm on May 28, 2020

Language: English

Kubernetes (K8s) is an open-source system for automating deployment, scaling, and management of containerized applications.

In this tutorial, you will learn the basics of Kubernetes, how it works, and its components. Starting from understanding its concepts and architecture, you will walk through different scenarios, deploying a single Pod, scaling it, persisting data, and much more.
