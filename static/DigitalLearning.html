Digital Learning.
An introduction to the world of digital learning tools and methods.

Join our interactive digital-only event series!

Discover the possibilities of digital learning in this online only event by the Microsoft Campus Community. Learn about new tools and expand your knowledge of Microsoft Teams by listening to Andreas Schlüter, the initiator of "Teams macht Schule" to help schools with their steps into the digital world.
Now more than ever is the topic of digital learning a pressing issue. So dive in with us and test out different methods and approaches to create your virtual classroom.
This event is for everyone who's interested, from teachers and professors to students or to none of the above. No prior knowledge is required.
Most of the sessions will be held in German. We will, however, provide english subtitles.
