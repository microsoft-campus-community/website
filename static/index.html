Welcome to the Microsoft Campus Community

The Community connects tech interested and open-minded students who want to develop their skillset. Our goal is to engage with people who share our passion. Together, we learn and build a strong network empowering each other to achieve more.

It is not all about coding. We want you to connect with peers and industry experts. Foster your network, learn new technologies and accelerate your personal growth. To be a future leader in tech, you should be open to share expertise as well as experience and inspire others to live up to their potential.

Together

Meet fantastic people and get to know open-minded students in your region. Easily find members who share your passion. Use the opportunity to learn and pass on knowledge, to try out new technologies and develop awesome projects.

& With Others

We strive to connect with inspiring people outside the community. Learn about cutting edge technologies from industry experts and share your acquired knowledge. Get involved, connect with people and organize spectacular events at your campus to inspire others.

A Team of Dreamers

Our Community brings diverse people together. We are motivated and enthusiastic students who share our passion for technology with others.
