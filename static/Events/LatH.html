Introspecting app performance in Kubernetes

Furqan Shakoor and Sahil Kalra from Motius will talk about understanding the performance aspects of Kubernetes, how to get visibility into the cluster performance and how to get the most out of your hardware.

Why do my pods keep dying?

We will look beyond the cluster abstractions and see how docker containers get scheduled to run on individual machines. We will demonstrate some observability tools that can help us see the current state of the cluster. And we'll talk about some common problems and pitfalls. We will briefly talk about mitigation strategies for everyday issues as well. So, if your apps keep dying with an OOM and you don't know why this session is for you.
