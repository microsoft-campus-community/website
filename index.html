We connect an inclusive community of experts & events with visionary students who will change the future of IT.
We believe in 3 principles that will lead to innovative and successful results together with a cooperative work ethic:

Inspire

Get inspired by the world of IT: Develop new skills with the help of the Microsoft Campus Community. Start your own projects or support others by using your new skills.

Learn

Use the opportunity to expand your knowledge in unfamiliar terretories. Acquire new knowledge on your own or in a team through workshops and provided resources.

Develop

Build your network and get ready to start your career in the world of IT. Grow steadily to reach your fullest potential by staying curious and getting a better version of yours every day.
